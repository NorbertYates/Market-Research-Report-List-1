<p><p>Transparent overwrap films are commonly used for packaging various products. Shrink films are made from polymer materials that shrink when heat is applied, tightly conforming to the shape of the item being wrapped. This type of film provides excellent protection against moisture, dust, and tampering. On the other hand, stretch films are elastic and can be stretched around products to secure them tightly without the need for heat sealing. They are commonly used for bundling and palletizing goods. Both types offer transparency, protection, and convenience in different packaging applications.</p></p>
