HPLC (High-Performance Liquid Chromatography) separates and quantifies components in a mixture. GC-MS (Gas Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry) analyzes volatile compounds. These methods are used to detect contaminants, pesticides, allergens, pathogens, and other quality parameters in agricultural products.</p></p>
