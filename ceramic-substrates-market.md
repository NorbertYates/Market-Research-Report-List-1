Aluminum nitride offers high thermal conductivity and low thermal expansion, making it suitable for applications in the automotive and aerospace industries. Silicon nitride exhibits exceptional mechanical strength and thermal shock resistance, making it suitable for demanding applications. Beryllium oxide is known for its high thermal conductivity and low dielectric constant, making it ideal for high-frequency applications in the electronics industry.</p></p>
