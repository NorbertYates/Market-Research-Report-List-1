<p><p>Plastisol sealants are a type of adhesive material used to create a watertight and airtight seal in various applications. There are different types of plastisol sealants available in the market. </p><p>Solvent-based sealants are made by dissolving plastisol resins in organic solvents. These sealants provide excellent adhesion and flexibility, making them suitable for a wide range of applications.</p><p>Water-based sealants, on the other hand, use water as the main solvent. They are considered more environmentally friendly and have lower VOC emissions compared to solvent-based sealants.</p><p>Other types of plastisol sealants include radiation-cured sealants, which are cured using ultraviolet (UV) radiation or electron beams. These sealants offer quick curing times and high bond strength.</p><p>Overall, these various types of plastisol sealants fulfill different requirements and offer specific advantages for different applications.</p></p>
