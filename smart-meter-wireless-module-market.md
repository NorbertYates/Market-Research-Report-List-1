<p><p>The Smart Meter Wireless Module market is categorized into two types: LoRa and NB-IoT. LoRa stands for Long Range, and it is a wireless technology that enables long-distance communication with low power consumption. It is primarily used for IoT applications that require wide coverage. On the other hand, NB-IoT (Narrowband Internet of Things) is a low-power wide-area network technology optimized for IoT devices. It provides reliable and secure connectivity for smart meters and other IoT devices, using existing cellular networks. Both technologies play a significant role in enabling efficient communication and data collection for smart metering systems.</p></p>
