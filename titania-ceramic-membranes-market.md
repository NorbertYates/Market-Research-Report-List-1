<p><p>Titania Ceramic Membranes are used in various applications due to their unique properties. The market for these membranes can be divided into two types: Dense Membranes and Porous Membranes. Dense Membranes have very small pores that allow selective separation of molecules based on size and charge, making them ideal for applications like water purification and gas separation. On the other hand, Porous Membranes have larger pores that allow both selective separation and high permeability, making them suitable for applications like filtration and catalysis. These different membrane types cater to different needs in various industries.</p></p>
