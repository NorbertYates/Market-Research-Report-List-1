<p><p>An arrestor, also known as a lightning arrestor or surge arrestor, is a device used to protect electrical systems and equipment from damaging power surges and transient voltage spikes caused by lightning strikes or other electrical disturbances. It is designed to divert excessive electrical current to the ground, thereby preventing it from flowing into the protected equipment and causing damage.</p><p>The future outlook of the arrestor market is promising, driven by several factors. Firstly, the increasing demand for reliable power supply and the growing concern for the protection of electrical systems and devices from lightning strikes and power surges are major drivers of market growth. Additionally, the rising adoption of arrestors in various industries such as telecommunications, power utilities, transportation, and manufacturing further contributes to market expansion.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing emphasis on the development of advanced arrestor technologies, such as hybrid arrestors and smart arrestors, is expected to drive market growth in the coming years. These advanced arrestors offer improved protection capabilities, enhanced durability, and better monitoring and diagnostic features, thereby increasing their appeal to end-users.</p><p>Moreover, the increasing investments in infrastructure development projects, particularly in developing economies, are anticipated to boost the demand for arrestors. The expanding urbanization, rising population, and the need for reliable electrical systems in various sectors like residential, commercial, and industrial are driving investments in power distribution infrastructure, augmenting the demand for arrestors.</p><p>According to the provided information, the arrestor market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.5% during the forecast period.
