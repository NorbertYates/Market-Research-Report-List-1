<p><p>LVDT (Linear Variable Differential Transformer) position sensor is a type of electromechanical sensor used to measure linear displacement. It consists of a primary coil, two secondary coils, and a ferromagnetic core that moves within the coil assembly. As the core moves, it induces voltages in the secondary coils, and the differential output is proportional to the position of the core.</p><p>The LVDT position sensor market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The rising demand for accurate and reliable position sensing in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing is driving the market growth. LVDT sensors offer advantages like high precision, long lifespan, and resistance to harsh environments, making them suitable for a wide range of applications.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing adoption of automation and robotics in industries is further fueling the demand for LVDT position sensors.
