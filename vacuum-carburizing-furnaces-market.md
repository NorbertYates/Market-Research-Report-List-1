<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Vacuum carburizing furnaces are categorized into three types: single-chamber, double-chamber, and multi-chamber. Single-chamber furnaces consist of a single enclosure where the carburizing process takes place. Double-chamber furnaces have two separate chambers for the carburizing and quenching processes. Multi-chamber furnaces feature multiple chambers for various processes such as pre-heating, carburizing, and quenching, allowing for simultaneous or sequential heat treatments. These different types provide manufacturers with options to choose the most appropriate furnace configuration based on their specific production requirements and preferences.</p></p>
