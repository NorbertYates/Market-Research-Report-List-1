<p><p>An oxygen concentrator is a medical device used to deliver supplemental oxygen to individuals with low oxygen levels in their blood. It works by extracting air from its surroundings, removing nitrogen and other gases, and delivering purified oxygen through a nasal cannula or mask. This portable device is commonly used for patients suffering from respiratory disorders such as chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) and asthma.</p><p>The global oxygen concentrator market has experienced significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue this trend in the forecast period. Several factors are contributing to this growth, including an increasing geriatric population, rising prevalence of respiratory diseases, and technological advancements in oxygen concentrator devices.</p><p>The market analysis indicates that the rising number of people suffering from respiratory disorders and the growing demand for home healthcare solutions are the key drivers behind the market growth.
