<p><p>Automobile surface coating refers to the process of applying a protective layer on the external surfaces of vehicles, such as cars, motorcycles, and commercial vehicles. <p><p>The automobile surface coating market is divided into several types, including solvent-borne, water-borne, powder coating, and others. Solvent-borne coatings use organic solvents as a medium and provide excellent performance and durability. Water-borne coatings, on the other hand, use water as a medium and offer environmental advantages. Powder coatings involve the application of dry paint particles and provide a durable and attractive finish. Finally, the "others" category includes various coatings such as UV-cured coatings and high-solid coatings, which offer specific benefits and applications within the automobile surface coating market.</p></p>
