<p><p>The automotive thermoplastic resin composites market is divided into various types based on the reinforcement materials used. Glass Mat Thermoplastic (GMT) composites consist of randomly dispersed glass fibers for improved strength. Short Fiber Thermoplastic (SFT) composites have short fibers blended with thermoplastic resin for enhanced mechanical properties. Long Fiber Thermoplastic (LFT) composites have longer fibers providing higher strength and stiffness. Continuous Fiber Thermoplastic (CFT) composites have continuous fibers embedded in a thermoplastic matrix for optimal performance. The "Others" category consists of any other types of thermoplastic resin composites used in the automotive industry.</p></p>
